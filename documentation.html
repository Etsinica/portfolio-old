about me

Ethan Sinica

MAKER

The main intended audience of my portfolio website is potential employers. They want to see why they should hire me over any other applicant for their open position and I certainly want to show them this. Showing someone why they should hire me involves more than just a display of my work. I should be able to portray personality and some aspect that makes my portfolio stick in their head (for good reasons).

Initially, for V1 of my portfolio, I had some superfluous javascript based off of P5.JS code on their website. They coded the classic game of “Snake” and I adapted the code for drawing the snake into a mouse based drawing background application. It was useless I suppose. Now the website is just my own code. I suppose W3Schools helped a fair bit but most of that just about falls under common knowledge.

This project, on my second go of it, really helped me learn how to actually position content in web design. It was so gratifying seeing everything fit how it did, be responsive, and essentially, just sit there how I envisioned! The initial idea was to work in an AR.JS business card portion to the website but I am glad that I focused on the core website alone.

I’ll email you a copy of the business card some time over break when I make it; it’s a fun little thing that I want to share with people!
